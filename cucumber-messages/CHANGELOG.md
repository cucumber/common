### Fixed

## [2.1.0] - 2018-11-01

### Added

  ([#488](https://github.com/cucumber/cucumber/pull/488)
   [brasmusson])

## [2.0.0] - 2018-10-14

### Added

* Several messages to support [cucumber-engine]()
  ([#502](https://github.com/cucumber/cucumber/pull/502)

### Added
